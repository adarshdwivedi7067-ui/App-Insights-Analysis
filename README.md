📱 Google Play Store Apps: Market & Insights Analysis

## Project Overview
Mobile app performance and user engagement are critical factors in today’s digital ecosystem.  
This project conducts a comprehensive analysis of **9,367 apps** from the Google Play Store dataset using **Looker Studio**.  
The objective was to identify market composition, user behavior, and monetization strategies, and provide **data-driven recommendations** for app developers, marketers, and product teams.

---

## Business Problem
The mobile app market is highly competitive, with billions of installs and thousands of apps competing for user attention.  
Key business questions addressed in this analysis include:

- **Market Composition:** Which categories dominate the app ecosystem?  
- **Pricing Strategy:** How do free vs. paid apps perform in terms of installs and engagement?  
- **User Sentiment:** What role do ratings and reviews play in app success?  
- **Top Performers:** Which apps and categories lead in installs and user engagement?  

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset covers **9,367 apps** with features such as:

- **App Metadata:** Category, Type (Free/Paid), Content Rating.  
- **Performance Metrics:** Installs, Reviews, Ratings.  
- **Market Distribution:** Category-wise app counts and installs.  

Target analysis: Identify **patterns and insights** that explain app success and user engagement.

---

## Methodology & Technical Stack
**Technologies Used:**
- **Tool:** Google Looker Studio  
- **Preprocessing:** SQL / Excel  
- **Visualization:** Interactive dashboards (charts, tables, KPI cards)  

**Analytical Approach:**
- **Data Cleaning:** Ensured consistency in installs, ratings, and reviews.  
- **Univariate Analysis:** Category distribution, free vs. paid apps.  
- **Bivariate Analysis:** Correlation between installs, ratings, and reviews.  
- **Dashboarding:** Built interactive visualizations for stakeholders.  

---

## Key Findings (Summary)
- **Free Apps Dominate:** 92.6% of apps are free, driving maximum installs.  
- **Games Lead Installs:** Subway Surfers (6bn installs), Candy Crush Saga (3bn), 8 Ball Pool (600m).  
- **Sports Apps:** ESPN, CBS Sports, Bleacher Report show strong engagement but lower installs compared to games.  
- **User Ratings Matter:** Average rating of 4.19 indicates positive sentiment; higher ratings correlate with higher installs.  
- **Category Insights:** Family and Communication apps also contribute significantly to overall installs.  

---

## Recommendations
- **Developers:** Focus on free apps with monetization strategies (ads, in-app purchases).  
- **Marketing Teams:** Prioritize promotion in high-install categories like Games and Communication.  
- **Product Teams:** Improve ratings via UX updates and regular feedback loops.  
- **Business Strategy:** Explore niche categories (Medical, Finance, Lifestyle) where competition is lower but demand is growing.
